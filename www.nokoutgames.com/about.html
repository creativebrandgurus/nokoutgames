Our Vision

What Drives Us?

What Drives Us?

01

Passion

Our core driving factor is our passion towards games, technology, art and business around it. With Passion at the heart, we nurture ideas, inspire excellence, and find creative ways to entertain the global audience.

02

Creators DNA

Thinking differently is in our DNA. We are proud creators at a core. We approach our works in sense of wonder and imagination. Every day is an opportunity to add a pinch of invention and innovation to this world.

03

Team Sprit

We are bonded by the game we play and game we want to develop. We celebrate and embrace our diversity. We invest in each other because we win or lose as a team. In the process we collaborate, communicate and create great games.

04

Honesty

We are best as our self. We bring our perspective, challenges, approaches and solutions without any hesitations. We do what we say we’ll do. We believe truth is god and we stay close to it!

05

Adventures

We constantly challenge our work and produce next best product. We move quickly and we’re not afraid to make mistakes. Here, smart risk-taking is encouraged—even if it fails—and every team member is empowered to tackle challenges in exciting and innovative ways.

06

We own our #hits

We take accountability for your deeds. We are our own CEOs, we take charge of our situations and make sure the project and company moves forward. Waiting for some feedback? We will go and get it! Want something done? We will do it

07

No Limit

We aim high and create opportunities for ourselves, team and the company. We’re constantly developing, testing, and improving to make sure we’re delivering the best games. We never settle because we believe there’s always an opportunity to do better.

08

Fun

We enjoy the journey. We crave for new experience and cherish life!
